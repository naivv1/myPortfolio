Story
The game is a chapter-based that has Interactive 2D Storytelling Animation elements to enhance engagement and maintain the attention of young viewers, particularly children with ADHD.
Project Details
This is our capstone project. My groupmates created this project. My groupmates were the ones who made the assets and animations, while I'm the developer of the game and the interactions. I had lots of fun making this game because the panels liked it so much.
